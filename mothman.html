Mothman

Homoheterocerus occidentavirginianus

This well-known cryptid is a creature of the air; a tall humanoid with glowing red eyes, black feathering or fur, and avian features, often depicted with moth-like wings, a stooped posture, and a screeching call.

Mothman was first sighted in Point Pleasant, West Virginia, USA, though it has been found in Moscow, Russia, foreshadowing the 1999 Russian apartment bombings.

Story/Context

On November 15, 1966, Mothman was first spotted by two couples at Point Pleasant, who said that they encountered a seven-foot-tall black creature with white wings and a human-like stature. It flew after them until the Point Pleasant city limits as they drove away.
It has been sighted a few times after this, and may be responsible for the collapse of the Silver Bridge in 1967, which killed 46 individuals.
Those who refuse to believe in the mothman blame the sightings on sandhill cranes and green herons.
